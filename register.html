ABOUT FRUIT MASTER

Fruit Master Farms Farmer’s Market is the foremost weekly organic farmer’s market in Nigeria with a focus on organic, natural, wellness and sustainable products. Fruit Master Farms is People’s Market is a not-for-profit initiative providing access to markets at an affordable rate for women-owned and women-led start-ups and early-stage agribusinesses. It is a socio-economic empowerment platform for women, offering a collaborative space that supports indigenous small business owners as they venture into their next business goal. It is held every Saturday at our flagship in various farms with across the nation. When you shop at the Agrip Farms Farmer’s Market, you are not only supporting women-owned brands but you are also contributing to the local economy as all the products are locally farmed, locally sourced, locally produced and handmade. Shop intentionally, shop purposefully, and shop locally! Let us work together as a community for a healthier local economy and a wealthier social community..
